The Grade Myth

"Ceremonial Grade" is an industry convention, not a regulated legal standard. True quality is defined by harvest time and processing. We use only first-harvest leaves.

The Region Myth

While Uji is famous, Shizuoka—the "Tea Capital of Japan"—offers superior terroir. Our matcha is 100% sourced from the volcanic soil of Shizuoka.

The Integrity Myth

Repackaging often leads to oxidation. Direct-from-Japan packaging ensures the product remains pristine. We guarantee 100% "Made in Japan" integrity with zero local repackaging.
